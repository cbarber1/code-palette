About me

Hi, my name is Chris Barber and I am a college student studying computer science. As you can probably tell, I love creative coding and generative art. I just find it fascinating that math and technology and randomness can create something so beautiful. I've only been doing generative art for a few months, but I decided to make this website to showcase my pieces along with their code which I think is art in itself (not that my code is any good haha).

I started on my generative and creative coding journey through the youtube channel The Coding Train. His videos are absolutely amazing and are great for both beginners and experts alike.

Hope you like it!
